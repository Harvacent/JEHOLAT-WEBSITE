About Us

About Jeholat Schools

Jeholat Schools started in September, 2013. The pioneer teacher is Miss Adeyemo Rachael Oluwatosin, Miss Ogidan Tosin and the Proprietor. These three people led the school to a commendable state. Before the completion of the first session, the school was able to admit a total number of 17 students. Jeholat Schools was established to empower students to acquire and demonstrate the value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners. The school has been approved by Early Child Care Development Education (ECCDE) and Osun State Ministry of Education. The schools is also registered under NBTE to run private technical schools and also licensed to observe NABTEB Examinations.

OBJECTIVES

Our Vission and Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, also to place our students on the pedestal of self reliance, self actualization and upright character.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enable learners' access to learning through the provision of differentiated, in-depth and cohesive learning programs and processes ran by highly effective teachers, focused on improving student outcomes.
