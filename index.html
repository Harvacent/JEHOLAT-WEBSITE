WHO ARE WE?

Welcome to Jeholat Schools

Jeholat Schools was established in September, 2013 to empower students to acquire and demonstrate the value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners. The school has been approved by Early Child Care Development Education (ECCDE) and Osun State Ministry of Education. OUR CORE VALUES

Character and Academic Development

We place equal emphasis on character and academic development. We believe that academic success alone is not sufficient for personal and professional growth. By prioritizing character and academic development, we aim to equip our students with the tools necessary to excel academically and lead fulfilling lives.

Excellence

We teach our students to always strive for the best in everything they do, academically, personally, and morally.

Discipline

We communicate to our students the importance of hard work and consistency in achieving great results.

Creativity

We cultivate creativity in our students by providing activities and environment that fosters new ideas and solving problems.

Integrity

We instill strong ethical and moral principles by showing the students to act with honesty, empathy and integrity.

Respect

We encourage our students to be respectful to everyone they come across, regardless of background or status.

Confidence

We believe that every child is a future leader and we encourage them to be self-confident about the person they want to become.

Why Jeholat Schools?

We offer differentiated, in-depth and cohesive learning programs and processes ran by highly effective teachers who are focused on improving student outcomes, through their commitment to professional development, quality teaching, evidence based practices, coaching and mentoring and collaboration. Our students are nurtured to participate and contribute to the development of the society they find themselves while practicing the core values of the school. We also ensure a quality inclusive learning environment that is responsive to student voice while enriching and engaging students for both mental and personal growth.

OBJECTIVES

Our Vission and Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, also to place our students on the pedestal of self reliance, self actualization and upright character.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enable learners' access to learning through the provision of differentiated, in-depth and cohesive learning programs and processes ran by highly effective teachers, focused on improving student outcomes.
