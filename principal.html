About Our Principal

Mrs. Adebanjo Janet Temilola is the Principal of Jeholat Schools, Ileogbo and an indigen of Ilesha, Osun state, Nigeria. She was born into the family of late G. M. Ajiwe and Mrs. Florence Ajiwe. She was the Head Girl during her study in secondary school. A born teacher, graduate of education, mother of toddlers, she worked at Ultimate Nursery and Primary Schools Ilesa before she joined the civil service (Customary Court of Appeals) an arm of Osun state judiciary on January 2005. She is very dedicated to work due to her passion for teaching. She joined forces with her husband (Mr. Adebanjo O Adeyemo) to found Jeholat Schools Ileogbo.
