About Our Proprietor

Mr. Adebanjo Olusuyi Adeyemo is the Proprietor of Jeholat Schools, Ileogbo and an indigene of Ilesha, Osun state, Nigeria. He was born into the family of Mr. Adewole Adeyemo and Mrs. Foluke Adeyemo. Mr. Adeyemo is a member of the Democratic Socialist Movement (DSM) and also a member (Secretary) of the Press Council. During his National Youth Service Corps (NYSC), he was the Cadet in Chief (CinC) of the Federal Road Safety Corps. Mr. Adebanjo Olusuyi Adeyemo is a Pastor in the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG). He is presently the Chairman of Association of Progressive Private Schools (APPS) Ayedire Chapter. He is also the State Coordinator for Osun State Progressive Unified Examination (OSPUE), the examination body of the Association of Progressive Private Schools (APPS) Osun state. He is married and blessed with godly seeds.
