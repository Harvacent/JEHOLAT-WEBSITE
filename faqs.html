Frequently Asked Questions

We have collated a list of some of our most Frequently asked questions. Do well to read through perhaps you might find direct answers to the questions you have in mind for jeholat schools.
Have any other unanswered question? Feel free to reach out to us. our representatives are willing to provide answers always.

Is Jeholat Schools an accredited school?

Jeholat Schools is a registered and accredited school, approved by Early Child Care Development Education (ECCDE) and Osun State Ministry of Education. The schools is also registered under NBTE to run private technical schools and also licensed to observe NABTEB.

Is Jeholat Schools an outstanding school?

Jeholat Schools was established to empower students to acquire and demonstrate the value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners. Yes! Jeholat Schools is an outstanding school.

What is the name of the Proprietor and Proprietress of Jeholat Schools?

The name of the Proprietor of Jeholate is Mr. Adebanjo Olusuyi Adeyemo. The name of the Proprietress is Mrs. Adebanjo Janet Temilola.

How do I get to Jeholat Schools?

Jeholat Schools is located at Behind Luther Kings College, Obate Area, Ileogbo , Osun state, Nigeria. You can contact us or send us a message for more information.

What year was Jeholat Schools established?

Jeholat Schools, Obate Area, Ileogbo was founded in September, 2013.

What is the school structure of Jeholat Schools?

Jeholat Schools is a school with both Primary and Secondary school. We have over 170 students currently in session.

Why Jeholat Schools?

We offer differentiated, in-depth and cohesive learning programs and processes ran by highly effective teachers who are focused on improving student outcomes, through their commitment to professional development, quality teaching, evidence based practices, coaching and mentoring and collaboration. Our students are nurtured to participate and contribute to the development of the society they find themselves while practicing the core values of the school. We also ensure a quality inclusive learning environment that is responsive to student voice while enriching and engaging students for both mental and personal growth.
